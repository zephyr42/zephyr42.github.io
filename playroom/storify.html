Storify

I love Twitter. The platform introduced me the online community of librarians and educators and has helped me learn from my fellow students outside the classroom. One of my favorite uses of Twitter is to provide a backchannel during events and seminars, and I make a point to create Storify collections of conversations which have had an effect on me and seminars where I could not keep up with the Twitter feed and wished to return in the future. In creating these collections, I've found that others use them as well, so I keep my Storify profile public and open for others to use as reference.
